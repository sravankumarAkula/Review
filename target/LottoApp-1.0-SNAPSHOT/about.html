How to Play?

Playing Indian lotto is as simple as cutting your favourite cake. You can win lot of money everyday just with few clicks. Register and Login. Once you login you will be redirected to available lottos page automatically. You will see table of contents with lottery details like Amount To Pay , Winning Amount , Last Day To Register, Lotto Draw Date , Lotto Status and Your Status.


If you are interested in any lottery, pay the amount you need to register for that lottery using paytm and once done click activate button beside that lottery and enter the transaction number you received on paytm. After that you will see status as Sent to Approval. If your transaction is success, our management will approve your request and you are registered for that lottery. On draw date, you will see the result beside that lottery.
